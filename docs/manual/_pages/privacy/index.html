HDZap Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

HDZap does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information the app handles stays entirely on your device.

Data We Do Not Collect

HDZap does not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Device identifiers
  • Any data sent to external servers

Local Storage

Race history (lap times, race records) is saved locally in your iPhone's Application Support directory. This data is included in standard iOS backups if you have iCloud or local backup enabled — it is never uploaded to any server controlled by the developer.

Bluetooth

HDZap uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to communicate with your M5StickS3 bridge device. No data is transmitted to the developer or any third party through this connection. The connection is direct, device-to-device only.

Third-Party SDKs

HDZap contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection SDKs.
